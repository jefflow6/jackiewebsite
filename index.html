COUNSELING FOR INDIVIDUALS, CHILDREN AND FAMILIES

I am a Marriage and Family Therapist who provides therapy in a warm and caring manner. My practice focuses on helping Individuals resolve past problems and improve their lives. I have experience working with children, adolescents, and families build strong relationships. I have experience helping children reduce emotional and behavior problems. I have been trained in using a variety of therapeutic approaches. I use a client-centered approach where you are the expert in your own life. Individual and Couples Therapy Services

I provide Individual therapy services for people struggling with managing difficult life experiences. I have experience in helping people resolve problems and reduce symptoms that are impacting their ability to move forward in their lives.

Clinical Supervision
